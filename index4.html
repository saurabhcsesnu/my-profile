About Me

I am an Assistant Professor and Principal Investigator focusing on the intersection of Applied Mathematics and Artificial Intelligence. My research is heavily oriented toward advanced Computer Vision and Deep Learning, with a specific focus on developing and analyzing architectures such as R-CNN, DETR, DINO, and I-JEPA.

Through my research group, we aim to leverage high-performance GPU computing to solve complex modeling and visual processing challenges.
