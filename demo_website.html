About Me

Dr. Amelia Hayes is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Tech. Her research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science, with applications in healthcare and environmental sustainability. She is dedicated to advancing knowledge and mentoring the next generation of computer scientists.

Research Interests

Dr. Hayes's research interests include: - Machine Learning - Artificial Intelligence - Data Science - Healthcare Applications - Environmental Sustainability
